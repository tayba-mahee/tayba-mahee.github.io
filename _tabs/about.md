## About Me

I am a Computer Science educator and researcher currently serving as an Adjunct Lecturer at Baylor University in Waco, Texas. My work focuses on the intersection of technology and education, with research interests spanning Human-Computer Interaction, Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and User Research.

## Education

Baylor University, Waco, Texas (2023)

**Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering**

## Research Interests

- **Human-Computer Interaction**: Exploring how cultural differences impact information-seeking behavior and website design
- **Machine Learning**: Developing models for healthcare diagnostics, traffic prediction, and environmental monitoring
- **Computer Vision**: Applying deep learning techniques to satellite imagery and medical imaging
- **User Research**: Investigating culturally adaptive design and user experience optimization

## Current Role

As an Adjunct Lecturer at Baylor University, I teach undergraduate computer science courses including Discrete Structures and Introduction to Computer Systems. I am passionate about implementing adaptive and student-centered teaching strategies that promote conceptual understanding and problem-solving skills.
